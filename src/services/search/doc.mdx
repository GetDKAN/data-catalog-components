# Search Services

## Reducers
Below is the list of possible reducers available in the Search component.
1. **UPDATE_SORT:** Sets **sort** to the value passed through `action.data.sort` and sets **sort-order** to the value passed through `action.data['sort-order']`.
1. **UPDATE_FULLTEXT:** Sets **fulltext** to the value passed through `action.data.fulltext`.
1. **UPDATE_PAGE_SIZE:** Sets **page-size** to the value passed through `action.data['page-size']` and resets **page** to `1`.
1. **UPDATE_CURRENT_PAGE:** Sets **page** to the value passed through `action.data.page`.
1. **UPDATE_FACETS:** Sets **selectedFacets** to the facets passed in through `action.data.selectedFacets` and sets **page* to `1`.
1. **RESET_FULLTEXT:** Sets **fulltext** to `''`.
1. **RESET_FACETS:** Sets **selectedFacets** to the facets passed in through `action.data.selectedFacets` and sets **page* to `1`.
1. **RESET_ALL:** Sets **fulltext** to `''` and **selectedFacets** to `[]`.
